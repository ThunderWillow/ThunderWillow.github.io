My Foster Kittens

Hello! Welcome to this (quote quote) website. I'm fostering kittens that are adoptable through Oregon Friends of Shelter Animals. (OFOSA) Here are some pictures and descriptions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------ ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • VESPER
  • Around 1 years old
  • Female

Vesper is the mom to all the kittens. She is a Dilute Tortiseshell Mix. Vesper loves people and is super sweet. She is adoptable through OFOSA and needs a forever home!

----------------------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • MIDNIGHT
  • 9 weeks old
  • Female

Midnight is a little Tuxedo Mix kitten. She likes to snuggle up with people and loves to petted when she's laying down. She purrs whenever she is petted when she's laying down. Midnight is adoptable through OFOSA and needs a forever home!

--------------------------------------------------------------------------- -------------------------------------------------------------------------

  • LIGHTNING
  • 9 weeks old
  • Male

Lightning is a Persian/Tabby Mix kitten. He is a bit shy and will need to get to know you a bit before he opens up and starts purring. He gets easily scared due to his shyness, but he really is a sweet kitten once you get to know him! Lightning is adoptable through OFOSA and needs a forever home!

---------------------------------------------------------------------------- ---------------------------------------------------------------------

  • TIGERLILY
  • 9 weeks old
  • Female

TigerLily is a Calico Mix kitten. She is the craziest out of the three. She loves to jump around and play with cat toys and just run around. She loves to bat at things with her paw and is not shy at all. TigerLily is adoptable through OFOSA and needs a forever home!
